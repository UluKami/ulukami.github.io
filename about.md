Hello! My name is Kristian Valuyskiy!

I am a graduate with a bachelor of science majored in Information Technology. 
I have netted my AWS Cloud Foundations certification, but I am still striving towards achieving my goal of obtaining a AWS CloudOps Engineer certification. 
I am deeply interested in transforming on-premises architecture into the ever growing cloud space. 
I have previous experience in design from my vocational schooling and have a background in computer assembly.

After obtaining my degree, I am still applying and expanding my knowledge further through personal projects.
As of current, I have worked on setting up a closed environment for students to practice their Cyber Security skills, tinkering with Docker to host video game servers from my network, and developing on-premise to cloud work environments.

I have been tinkering and finding out about the inner workings of computers ever since I decided to build a computer for myself. 
This drive to learn more about the system that I use has driven me to explore and expand my knowledge about the capabilities that a computer can reach.
This passion of mine for computers has led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology and focus my studies into the inner workings of these machines.
